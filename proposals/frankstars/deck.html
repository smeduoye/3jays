Design philosophy

Built for trust, not takeover

You approve everything

No automatic bookings. Every outbound proposal passes through you — approve, edit, or reject in seconds.

Customers say yes

A slot isn't allocated until the client confirms. A real handshake: your proposal, their acceptance.

Flexible when quiet

Off-peak floating windows let clients stay loose while you pack the day the way you want.
